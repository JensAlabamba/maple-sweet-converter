Frequently Asked Questions

Do you store my images?

No. Uploaded files are deleted after conversion and ZIP delivery.

How long are files kept?

Only during processing and handoff. Cleanup runs after the ZIP delivery flow completes.

What formats are supported?

HEIC, HEIF, PNG, WEBP, JPEG, and JPG.

Is there a limit on uploads?

Yes. The limit is 20MB per file, up to 500 files in one batch, which works out to roughly 10GB total per batch.

What happens after a paid checkout?

Your success page verifies the checkout session, then you return to finalize the prepared batch.
